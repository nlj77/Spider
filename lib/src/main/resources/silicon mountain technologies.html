who is smt?

we are a group of unique, domestic engineers, product managers, project managers, consultants, and solutions architects. the company started in 2001 when the founders decided to hang up the corporate, fortune 50 lifestyle for a self-driven path. in the past twenty years, smt has built a developer toolkit and several product lines, learning from each and every success, and even more from the failures. we pride ourselves on our low turnover, and a real 40 hour workweek.

we approach every problem with a critical mind, asking "why" rather than simply following orders. our expertise is in designing and building the right solution, not the first one the business contemplates. let us take on that burden and build you a scalable, reliable solution.
